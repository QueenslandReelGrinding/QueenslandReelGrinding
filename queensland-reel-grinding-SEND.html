About Us

Queensland Reel Grinding is a specialist reel mower grinding service proudly servicing golf courses, sports fields, councils, schools, turf farms and commercial turf managers throughout Queensland and Northern New South Wales.

We understand that quality of cut is critical to turf health, presentation and playing performance. That's why we utilise industry-leading grinding equipment and proven techniques to deliver precision spin grinding and relief grinding services that restore reels and bedknives to the highest possible standard.

Using world-class grinding technology, we ensure every reel is accurately sharpened and correctly set — producing a cleaner cut, healthier turf and improved mower performance. Whether you manage golf greens, sporting surfaces, large commercial turf areas or a private property, our focus is on helping you achieve the best possible results from your equipment.

Our commitment to quality workmanship, attention to detail and reliable service has made Queensland Reel Grinding a trusted partner for turf professionals across the region.
